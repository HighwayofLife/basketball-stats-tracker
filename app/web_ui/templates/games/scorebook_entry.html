Scoring Notation Guide
Shot Types:
  • 1 = Made Free Throw
  • x = Missed Free Throw
  • 2 = Made 2-Point Shot
  • - = Missed 2-Point Shot
  • 3 = Made 3-Point Shot
  • / = Missed 3-Point Shot
Examples:
  • 22-1x = 2 made 2pts, 1 missed 2pt, 1 made FT, 1 missed FT
  • 3/2 = 1 made 3pt, 1 missed 3pt, 1 made 2pt
  • 11 = 2 made free throws
  • -/ = 1 missed 2pt, 1 missed 3pt
